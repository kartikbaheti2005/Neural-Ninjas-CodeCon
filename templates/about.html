About EcoGraph

EcoGraph is a platform dedicated to visualizing ecological data.


Hey there, you seem to be curious about the current environmental status. So, congratulations, you are on to a good visual and understanding treat.

Welcome to EcoGraph — a project I created to bring real-time environmental insights to everyone. With the help of `NASA Earth Data APIs, I've built a platform that focuses on India's environmental health, combining science with simplicity.

Here's what you can explore on EcoGraph:

  1. ⁠Live Temperature Updates

    2. I show real-time temperature data across different Indian states, so you can stay informed about weather conditions as they change. I provide live temperature readings across all Indian states.     3. Curious about how much carbon is being released in your area? I’ve made it easy to track emissions and understand their impact.
